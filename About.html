About Nick Fisichella

Greetings,

My name is Nick Fisichella and I am the owner & operator of Manna Media Solutions. I have been an artist my entire life and I am eager to use my abilities to help other individuals and businesses flourish and prosper.

I am from Pinellas Park, Florida and I've lived in the Tampa Bay Area since the day I was born. I love to grow gardens and make music but most of all, I love to learn.

I am thankful to everyone that has supported me - family, friends and clients. Most importantly, I am thankful for God for blessing me with certain gifts and talents. I want to do my best to honor the purpose of my gifts by serving others and serving God.

I hope to hear from you soon!

- Nicholas Francis Fisichella
